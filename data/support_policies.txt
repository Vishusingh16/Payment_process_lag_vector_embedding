[PAYMENT_FAILURE]

Issue: UPI Timeout

Description:
The customer completed the UPI payment, but the merchant did not receive confirmation.

Resolution:
Wait up to 30 minutes for automatic reconciliation.
If the payment remains unresolved after 24 hours, raise a support ticket.

[PAYMENT_FAILURE]

Issue: Insufficient Balance

Description:
The customer's bank account does not contain sufficient funds.

Resolution:
Ask the customer to verify account balance and retry the payment.
No amount is debited in this scenario.

[PAYMENT_FAILURE]

Issue: Card Authentication Failed

Description:
The customer failed OTP verification or 3D Secure authentication.

Resolution:
Retry the transaction with correct authentication details.
After multiple failures, contact the issuing bank.

[PAYMENT_FAILURE]

Issue: Bank Server Unavailable

Description:
The issuing bank's systems are temporarily unavailable.

Resolution:
Wait a few minutes and retry the transaction.
If the issue persists, use an alternative payment method.

[REFUND]

Issue: Refund Processing Time

Description:
A merchant has initiated a refund request.

Resolution:
Refunds are generally processed within 5-7 business days depending on the payment method and bank.

[REFUND]

Issue: Failed Refund

Description:
The refund attempt was rejected by the bank.

Resolution:
The payment gateway retries the refund automatically.
If the refund continues to fail, the support team contacts the customer.

[DISPUTE]

Issue: Unauthorized Transaction

Description:
The customer claims the transaction was not authorized.

Resolution:
The customer should immediately report the issue.
The dispute team reviews authentication logs and transaction history.

[DISPUTE]

Issue: Chargeback Initiated

Description:
The issuing bank has initiated a chargeback against the transaction.

Resolution:
The merchant must provide supporting evidence within the specified timeline.
The dispute team evaluates the evidence before responding.

[PAYMENT_FAILURE]

Issue: Payment Marked Failed but Amount Debited

Description:
The customer sees a failed transaction status but money has been deducted from the account.

Resolution:
Wait for reconciliation.
Most transactions are automatically reversed within 5 business days.
If not reversed, raise a support request with transaction details.

[DISPUTE]

Issue: Product Not Received

Description:
The customer claims that the purchased product was never delivered.

Resolution:
The merchant should provide shipment tracking details and proof of delivery.
If delivery cannot be verified, a refund may be issued.

[REFUND]

Issue: Duplicate Payment

Description:
The customer was charged twice for the same order.

Resolution:
Verify transaction records.
One of the duplicate transactions should be refunded after validation.
